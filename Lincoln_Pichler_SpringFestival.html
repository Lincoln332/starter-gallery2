Campbellsport Spring Festival Announced for June

Exciting event promises fun for the whole community

The town of Campbellsport is gearing up for its annual Spring Festival, scheduled for the first weekend of June. This year promises to be bigger and better, with new activities and local vendors participating.

"The festival is a great way to bring our community together," said event coordinator Sarah Johnson. We have live music, food trucks, games, and even a petting zoo for kids.
